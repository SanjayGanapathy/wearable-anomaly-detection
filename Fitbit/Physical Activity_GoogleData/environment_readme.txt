Time Series Data Export

The Time Series export provides a detailed timeline of your tracked activity.

Files Included:
----------

environment_YYYY-MM-DD.csv            - Where YYYY-MM-DD is the starting date for the entries in the file.

Each entry has the following values:

    timestamp                         - Date and time at which the entry was logged.
    magnet exists                     - Whether the device detected the presence of a magnetic field. Values are true or false.
